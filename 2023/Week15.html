Phantom League Championship Quarter-Finals

And an exciting first week of the playoffs it was... the top two seeds both lost - one by only scoring 109 points (a pretty standard occurence for them really), the other despite scoring 140 points (which would have beaten every other team in the playoffs but the one they played). So off to the losers bracket for the Super Steves and the Flat Bastards and on to the next round for the ABarfalypse and the Prey Haters respectively.

The number 3 and 4 seeds performed to expectations taking down their lower seeded opponnents and now become the top two seeds, with the Weapons of really large, I mean amazingly big, Fish Destruction taking out the Porkflops and the Frozen Topiarys sneaking by, in the lowest scoring matchup of the week, the Related by Births (think the Phat aren't jealous / disymayed by that?).
