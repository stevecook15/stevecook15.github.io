Thursday, December 19th
Phantom League Championship Semi-Finals

Last season the 8th seeded team took out the 1st seed in the first round and took out the 2nd seed in the second round to reach the PFL Championship. Well, that exact scenario has played out again this year as the Coco's will play in the Phantom Bowl Spectacular for the their 1st ever title after they Chopped the 2nd seeded Pork. Last season the Iceotopes got hot at the right time and went to the Super Bowl where they were summarily disposed of by the Preds. And here we go again, as the Ice won their quarter and semi-final matchups, beating the Weapons of Bass Destruction this week, so will play in the Phantom Bowl Spectacular for the 2nd year in a row! Lots of nutty coincidences this season. So it'll be interesting to see if the Ice learned anything from last years experience, or if Coco can show experience doesn't matter at all in this battle of two 8-8 juggernauts.

In the Consolation Match, the Weapons of Bass Destruction, despite posting the low score of the week, will take on the #2 seeded Pork for chance at 3rd place moola.
