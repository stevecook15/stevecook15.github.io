As the old saying (sorta) goes, ya just gotta get to the dance because on any given sunday. Well, for three of the four 6-8 teams that barely squeaked their way into the playoffs, it was that Sunday! They took down three of the four top seeds to show that, pheh, the regular seasons overrated.

Stop me if you've heard this before - the number one seeded team, with the regular seasons best record, highest scoring averge and lowest opponent average, promptly loses in the first round of the playoffs to a suddenly hot 8th seed, who posts the high score for the week. No, I'm not talking about 2023 Phat Bastards, I'm talking about the 2024 Phat Bastards - it's like deja vu all over again. The Bastards are threatening to throw games next year just to avoid the number one seed! The Cocos are pretty excited about this of course because last years 8th seed went on to win the championship after smashing the Phat.

Meanwhile, the 6-8 Weapons of Shaheed Destruction posted the second highest score of the week to upset the She Sheds who picked the wrong week to post their lowest score of the year. Also meanwhile the 6-8 Iceolinear Totes had the third highest score of the week (I'm sensing a trend here...) to Nix Their Hopes (at least it was closer than the other two matchups.
